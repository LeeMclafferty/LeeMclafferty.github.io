About Me

Hi! my name is Lee.

I am a driven and passionate student software developer seeking my first role as a full-time programmer. With a lifelong interest in programming, I decided to take advantage of the extra time during the COVID-19 pandemic to pursue my passion more deeply.

I delved into the world of C++ by immersing myself in "Sam's Teach Yourself C++ In One Hour a Day," a 1000-page book that I read cover to cover. Armed with this knowledge, I created projects like a C++ hangman game and a feature-rich To-do list, where I explored nesting objects, data structures, algorithms, and the C++ STL.

Fueling my fascination for game development, I discovered the remarkable potential of Unreal Engine. Hours turned into days as I dedicated myself to mastering its tools, understanding the source code, and blending coding with visual scripting to create immersive gaming experiences.

In addition to my programming endeavors, I gained hands-on experience in server management and Linux terminal commands during my work at a company. There, I honed my Python skills, automating tasks and streamlining problem-solving processes.

Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in software engineering, I am eager to venture into the professional world of programming. My ultimate goal is to work with a team that values growth and intelligence, while pushing me to develop my skills as a C++ developer. I am open to exciting opportunities, and if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work, please explore the rest of my portfolio and feel free to reach out!


Let's create remarkable software and bring imaginative ideas to life!
